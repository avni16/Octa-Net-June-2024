About Us

History

I was 10 years old when I first read the novel "Matilda" by Roald Dahl. That book engaged me so much that I knew I will never be the same again. My 10-year-old self marked the beginning of being a book lover.

I decided to open my own bookstore when I was 17 in my final year of high school. To share the joy I felt while reading a novel and completing it was unmatched. I wanted to share this feeling with everyone who was also a book lover.

Mission

Our mission is to reach as many people as possible and give them the sources to read all the books they want.
